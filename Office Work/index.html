VOLUNTEER FEE

All Volunteers of REACH are required to make a
compulsory donation of US$300 towards our
operational costs. These funds are vital in enabling
us to pay our staff, and to sustain our programs.
In return you will receive:
• Assistance prior to your arrival
• Discounted rates at our partner hotels
• Comprehensive Volunteer Guidebook
• 1 x Free Airport transfor & town orientation
• Return transfer to school on your first day
• Enjoy daily nutritious meals with the kids
• Ongoing support throughout your stay

If you need any help setting up a fundraising page
let us know, we're here to help!
